Test Instructions

    Proctoring Guidelines to Ensure Integrity:

  • Choose a quiet room or place to attempt this video interview.
  • Ensure that there is no background noise, or nobody is talking when you are taking the interview.
  • Ensure that the test-taking area is free from distractions, and nobody other than you is appearing on your webcam during the interview.
  • Using of any phones, tablets, laptops, or any Bluetooth devices, that could potentially be used to access information or communicate with others during the interview is not allowed.
  • Keep looking at the camera and ensure there is good lighting while the video interview recording is in progress
  • Disabling camera or microphone is not allowed during the video interview.
  • Non-adherence to proctoring guidelines will impact your integrity score. Low integrity score with multiple violations would lead to rejection of your candidature for the selection process.

    • How to Navigate Questions:

  • You will get only one attempt to provide the answer to a question. You cannot go back to the previous questions during the interview.
  • Each question is timed. Timer will indicate the time left for the current question.
  • The system will automatically navigate to the next question when the time is over.
  • Final submission will be done automatically once the last question duration is completed.
